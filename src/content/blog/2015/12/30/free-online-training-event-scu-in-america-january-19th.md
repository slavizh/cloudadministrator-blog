Are you or your colleagues and clients interested in a free day of training on System Center and other Microsoft technologies? If so, I highly recommend that you check out [SystemCenterUniverse](http://www.systemcenteruniverse.com/) for the January 19th event. For background, this is an event is streamed live so it is available to be seen literally anywhere in the world with internet connectivity.

The first round of speaker information for SCU was recently released and is available at: [http://tinyurl.com/SCU2016Speakers](http://tinyurl.com/SCU2016Speakers) (The list includes Symon Perriman, Johan Arwidmark, Kent Agerlund, Dieter Wijckmans, Pete Zerger, Wally Mead and more!). For the most up-to-date speaker list check out: [http://www.systemcenteruniverse.com/presenters.htm](http://www.systemcenteruniverse.com/presenters.htm). Microsoft’s own [Jeremy Winters](https://channel9.msdn.com/Events/Speakers/Jeremy-Winter) will be delivering our keynote! Details next week when the session list goes live at: [http://www.systemcenteruniverse.com/](http://www.systemcenteruniverse.com/)

**Latest update**: The new CARL video about SCU 2016 is now online at: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7W6ZRYAFzDU](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7W6ZRYAFzDU)

Technologies included as topics at SCU 2016 in America include:

· Azure Automation

· Configuration Manager

· Hyper-V and Failover Clustering

· Intune

· IoT

· OMS

· Operations Manager

· Windows 10 Deployment

· Windows Server 2016

If you want to attend virtually, there are user group based simulcast locations around the world. Registration is available at: [http://www.systemcenteruniverse.com/venue.htm](http://www.systemcenteruniverse.com/venue.htm)

If you want to attend, the in-person event it is available in Dallas Texas and registration is available at: [https://www.eventbrite.com/e/scu-2016-live-tickets-7970023555](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/scu-2016-live-tickets-7970023555)

If you are interested in your user group hosting a simulcast location please reply back to myself and [scu@catapultsystems.com](mailto:scu@catapultsystems.com) to let us know and we will help to get you listed on our site simulcast site link at: [http://www.systemcenteruniverse.com/venue.htm](http://www.systemcenteruniverse.com/venue.htm)
